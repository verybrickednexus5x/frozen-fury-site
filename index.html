Frozen Fury

Welcome to the official website of the elite Scandinavian rock & heavy metal band, Frozen Fury!

Formed in the icy heart of Oslo, Frozen Fury is a powerhouse trio redefining modern Scandinavian metal. Known for their thunderous riffs, haunting melodies, and lyrics inspired by Norse mythology and modern chaos, they've carved a legendary path through Europe's underground scene.

Mr Fitzsimmons brings explosive energy on drums, while Mr Fitzsimmons' clone slays with face-melting guitar solos. And with Dr Jaxon Blake delivering vocals that flip between ethereal and demonic, their live shows hit like a blizzard straight from Valhalla.

Their debut song, 'Frozen Ground', shattered streaming records and their follow-up tour sold out arenas from Stockholm to Berlin. Whether you're a battle-hardened metalhead or just vibing to the roar of electric thunder — Frozen Fury will leave you frostbitten and begging for more.
